ABOUT

No.30 - Golden State Warriors

Wardell Stephen Curry II, born March 14, 1988, is an American basketball player and point guard for the Golden State Warriors in the NBA. Widely regarded as the greatest shooter ever, he revolutionized the game with his emphasis on three-point shooting.

Curry is a four-time NBA champion, two-time MVP, and holds multiple accolades, including Finals MVP, All-Star Game MVP, and Clutch Player of the Year. A ten-time NBA All-Star and scoring champion, he has also earned international success with gold medals at the FIBA World Cup and the 2024 Olympics.
